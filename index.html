On September 27th 2015, Arjun Anand Patel was born and the Arjun Wildlife Trust was established.

As a commemoration of his birth and his beautiful journey ahead, we established the Arjun Wildlife Trust to raise funds and provide design + technology resources for organizations around the world dedicated to preserving endangered wildlife.

About

As I write this, my son (to be named Arjun Anand Patel), is yet to be born. In his honor, my wife and I are launching the Arjun Wildlife Trust to deploy resources and funds around the world to help with conservation efforts and to combat poaching. I always told myself I would work hard to achieve financial independence and one day start a foundation to help the plight of many majestic species around the world. As I approach fatherhood at age 29, I realize the time is now. As populations of lions, tigers, elephants, rhinos and so many endangered species dwindle at an alarming rate, I know I don’t have the luxury of standing on the sidelines. My wife and I are personally committed to donating funds; our family business, Myriann, is committed to donating a percentage of profits each year; and we look to partner with our clients to gather additional resources and funds. All funds will go straight to the cause. Always.

This is also an experiment and exercise in parenting. I hope the Arjun Wildlife Trust will help my wife and I establish core values with our son – work ethic, accountability, transparency, compassion, and charity. We not only want to improve the dire situation with so many species but we also want to use the foundation as a teaching tool for our son. As he gets older, he’ll learn to raise and manage funds, build a team of passionate supporters, and travel with his parents to see the funds at work.

Whether this becomes his calling or not is to be determined. Frankly, it doesn’t matter. His birth has already inspired me to get started on a dream I’ve had since I was a kid. If he follows my footsteps, I’ll be proud. If he doesn’t, I’ll still be proud knowing that we gave him the tools he needed to succeed in something else.

We’re humbled by all the love and support we’ve received with this foundation and ask that you follow our journey – and Arjun’s! – to help preserve species around the world.

Anand Patel

Founder

Who We Are

The Arjun Wildlife Trust is a family-run foundation dedicated to providing funds
and resources for wildlife conservation. We look to help organizations around the
world in two ways

Digital Media

We donate web design and graphics design resources to organizations committed to conservation. A strong web presence is critical in raising awareness and fundraising. Throughout the year, we allocate many hours to supporting the people that are passionate about protecting our wildlife.

Funds

Each year, we donate a percentage of profits from our digital agency, Myriann. As our firm grows, we’ll contribute more to our partner organizations!

Our Partners

Our partners are carefully-selected organizations that share our passion for wildlife conservation. Our list will continue to grow as we discover more teams and causes that align with our goals!

Our Work

Maxwell

We’ve adopted Maxwell, a blind rhinoceros in Kenya that spends his time in the amazing care of the Sheldrick Wildlife Trust in Kenya. Our annual donation helps Maxwell get milk, food, medical attention, and the utmost in human care!

Run for Elephants

We donated a revamped website to the David Sheldrick Wildife Trust "Enormous Run for Elephants" event. Last year, they raised over $175,000 to get food and supplies for orphaned elephants and to actively patrol their habitats to combat poaching.
